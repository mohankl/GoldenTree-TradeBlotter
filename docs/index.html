What it does

📝

Trade Entry

Symbol, side, quantity, price — with validation that mirrors the server. Submits update the blotter instantly, no page reload.

📊

Live Blotter

All trades newest-first, sortable, with notional value and color-coded Buy/Sell pills for at-a-glance scanning.

📈

Positions

Net quantity and average cost per symbol, derived reactively. Net-zero symbols drop off automatically.

Average-cost on mixed buys / sells

Buy 100 @ 10, Buy 100 @ 12 → net 200 @ 11
Sell 50 @ 15 → net 150 @ 11 (avg unchanged — sell realizes P&L)
Sell 200 @ 20 → short 50 @ 20 (flip through zero resets avg cost)
